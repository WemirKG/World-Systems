Privacy & Responsible Use

Designed for real-world use with common-sense safeguards.

What we ask for

  • Email (to deliver access + updates)
  • Optional role info (to tailor onboarding)

What we recommend you do not share

  • Student or client PII (full names, IDs, addresses)
  • Medical, legal, or sensitive records
  • Passwords, private keys, account credentials

Important note

The Co-Pilot system is brand-led and delivered through trusted tools. Each underlying platform may have its own policies and data handling. We will always provide a clear “what to paste / what not to paste” checklist and teach safe usage patterns.

If you plan to deploy in a school or organization with strict compliance requirements, we can provide a pilot plan that reduces risk through anonymization workflows and restricted use cases.
